CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game


The are four crystals located below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click it.


Each time when the game starts, the value of each crystal will change as well as the random number.
